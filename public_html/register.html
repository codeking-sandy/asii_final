EXHIBITION

An exhibition of acoustic instruments and related articles would be held near the venue of the symposium. Stalls will be provided with table, two chairs and power supply with adequate lighting and a pedestal fan. Display items and charts to be brought by the firm booking the stall. Tariff for each stall is Rs 30,000/- plus GST as applicable (18% as of now). Firms are invited to utilize this opportunity to show case their products/articles to the delegates from all over India.
