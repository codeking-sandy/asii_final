    Welcome to the Acoustical Society of India, the professional society of acousticians. We are dedicated to promoting the study and understanding of acoustics in India. Acoustical Society of India (ASI) was established and registered at Visakhapatnam on 31st July, 1971 with the major objective of "The advancement of science & technology of all branches of acoustics". ASI conducts National Symposium on Acoustics annually to disseminate the latest trends in Acoustics among the professionals in the country. The Society has 30 local chapters and a combined membership of about 1140. It has 43 institutional members representing Indian Industry and 15 leading foreign experts as honorary fellows of the Society.

    Call for Papers

    IISA 2025 Gurugram invites papers related to various disciplines in Acoustics for presentation and publication in the proceedings. Selected articles will be published in Journal of Acoustical Society of India & Springer lecture proceedings. The papers must be original contributions of authors not published elsewhere. A technical committee will scrutinize the papers and decision of the committee will be final for acceptance of the paper.


    Topics of discussions are listed below:

    Architectural Acoustics, Aero Acoustics, Physical/Applied/Engineering/Vibro-Acoustics, Building & Environment Acoustics, Bio-acoustics, Noise Pollution and Control, Signal Processing in Acoustics, Speech, hearing and Music, Ultrasonics, Underwater Acoustics, Any other relevant topic.

    Submission of Abstract:

    The abstract shall be typed in the Times New Roman 12 font size with minimum 250 words and maximum of 500 words.

    Submission of Full Paper:

    On receipt of intimation of acceptance of abstract, author(s) can submit full paper in prescribed template. Necessary template and guidelines will be provided while intimating acceptance of abstract.

    Guidelines for paper submission

    • Experimental investigation with specific conclusions
    • Experimental investigation with specific conclusions
    • Case studies of industrial importance Simulation studies with practical demonstration/result
    • Design of instruments / Systems / Software

    Format for submission of paper

    • Paper should not exceed 8 (eight) A4 Size sheets
    • Font: Times New Roman
    • Margins: Left = 1.5 Inch
    • Right, Top & Bottom = 1.0 Inch
    • Abstract: 11 Point Italic with single line spacing to be placed after title and other details.
    • Key words: 4 (Four)
    • Text: 12 Point normal justified
    • Para heading: 12 Point Bold Sentence case,
    • Spacing = 1.5 lines, Figures to be inserted in jpg Format.
    • At least one of the authors is required to register and attend the conference for presenting the paper.
    • Maximum number of authors is 3 (Three)
    • E-mail of the corresponding author should be given.
